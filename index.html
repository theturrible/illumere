Andrey Grinkevich

Photography is a quest for beauty. Seeing of new places, finding a new perspective, and finally sharing with others – that’s what delights him.

Andrey is constantly looking for such places to transfer it’s beauty to photos to allow anyone to be there, to see it and feel the brilliance of life.

Andrey became interested in photography at an early age. Since then photography is not only a hobby for him but also a lifestyle. Wherever he is – his camera is always in his hands

Natalia Polyakova

Over а decade of photographing and playing with different techniques, Natalia found a balance between advanced technology and classical foundations of the photography. Her works are true magic of light happened in camera with only minimal post-processing. It makes every work unique.

“I’m going beyond the documentary snapshots to reflect the surrounding, smell, sound, mood, and history of the scene. We never see only a subject, but subject in the context. How we feel the subject depends on what we see around and what we saw before.” She said.
